About us

 Hack a life is a community centered around hacking, coding, and other computer science related topics.

Whether you're a working professional, a hobbyist, or a newbie starting out, we hope you'll enjoy yourself and find some value in our growing community.

Our aim is to help facilitate the discussion and learning of a wide range of computer science related topics. In the future we wish to introduce lessons, challenges/competitions, and other resources for those who wish to learn, no matter what your skill level is.

We actively discourage politics, non-constructive criticism, and elitism/ego tripping; we instead prefer personal development and enjoyable discussions.

Hack a life prescribes to the principle that hacking can be both fascinating and fun!
